About This Project

AI-powered healthcare innovation from Gujarat University

Project Mission

This project aims to leverage the power of Artificial Intelligence and Deep Learning to assist in the early detection of oral cancer. By analyzing medical images of oral cavities, our AI model can identify potential cancer indicators, providing a valuable tool for healthcare screening.

Early detection of oral cancer significantly improves treatment outcomes and survival rates. This tool is designed to support medical professionals and increase screening accessibility, particularly in resource-limited settings.

Technology Stack

This application is built using cutting-edge technologies in machine learning and web development:

TensorFlow 2.x
ResNet50
Flask
OpenCV
NumPy/Pandas

Academic Background

This project was developed as part of the Master of Science (M.Sc) program in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning at Gujarat University, India (2022-2024).

The research explores the practical application of Convolutional Neural Networks (CNN) and Transfer Learning in medical imaging, demonstrating how pre-trained models can be fine-tuned for specific healthcare tasks with limited training data.
